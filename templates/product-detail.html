Property Description

Beautiful Bungalow in the mountains of Rionegro with breathtaking views, authentic ranch character, attention to details and comfort truly offer an authentic experience. Disconnect from the city and get away. Medellin 45min, JMC International Airport & Llano Grande 20min, Guatapé/El Peñol 1 hour and much more.

Inside the Home

Open space bamboo cabin consisting of (1) bedroom, (1) living room, (1) full bathroom with hot water, fully equipped indoor kitchen with all basic necessities for cooking, large patio with beautiful garden overlooking mountain landscapes, and an outdoor grilling area.

This unique home offers an authentic Colombian experience, combined with Mexican themed decoration.
