Our Story

Born out of a passion for extraordinary living experiences, Vidalta was created to elevate the standard for vacation home rentals. We believe that a vacation is not just about visiting new places, but about fully immersing yourself in the experience, and that begins with an ideal place to call ‘home’.

Our journey is fueled by transparency, uncompromising quality, and a commitment to high service standards – values that are integral to our brand identity. We take great pride in crafting unforgettable vacation experiences while also positively contributing to the wellbeing and prosperity of the communities we serve.
